# Privacy Policy — Sleep & Calm

**Last updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview
Sleep & Calm ("the App") is a sleep and meditation application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect
- **Account data**: email address and display name (only if you sign in)
- **Usage statistics**: sleep streak, total listening time, active dates (stored locally and optionally synced to your account)
- **Favorites**: tracks and stories you mark as favorites

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide and improve the App's features
- To sync your progress across devices (if signed in)
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties
- We do **not** use your data for advertising

## Firebase
The App uses Google Firebase for authentication and data sync. Firebase may collect anonymized usage data. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Permissions
- **Internet**: required for account sync and sign-in
- **Notifications**: used only for the optional bedtime reminder you set
- **Boot completed**: used to restore your bedtime reminder after device restart

## Data Storage
- Your data is stored locally on your device
- If you sign in, data is synced to Firebase (Google Cloud, EU region)
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen

## Children
The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
